Our Story

I'm Hazel Smith, the proud founder of Jlyss Aesthetics and Laser. My journey in aesthetics is all about one thing: helping YOU feel beautiful and confident in your own skin.

I am a Licensed Medical Aesthetician, specializing in acne treatments, laser removal, cryotherapy and other aesthetic services. I'm deeply passionate about transforming lives through skincare. Every treatment I offer is tailored to bring ouut the best in you because everyone deserves to feel amazing!

Jlyss Aesthetics and Laser was born from a deep passion for helping people look and feel their best. Founded by Hazel Smith, a skincare expert specializing in several aesthetic services, this med spa was created with a mission to bring confidence and beauty to every patient. After years of witnessing the transformative power of effective skincare, Hazel decided to open a space where advanced technology and personalized care come together to achieve real, lasting results.

Whether you're battling acne, looking for smoother skin, or seeking recovery from muscle pain, we are here to provide expert solutions that cater to your unique needs. Jlyss Aesthetics and Laser is more than just a med spa - it's a place where your wellness is prioritized, and your beauty is celebrated. Welcome to your best self.
